background :

Projecting this service just as a sound financial choice will get us the attention of investors but in a country where only 1.5% of the population invests, that's very few people. A majority of the public sticks to savings accounts and F.D.s out of the distrust for and inexperience with financial institutions.

But loans are a household need and borrowers should be the prime target because investors value borrower involvement not vice-versa.

my suggestions:

1) We are not a good financial service from an exciting fintech startup. No.
We want to change the world for the better. We want to stop banks from exploiting people and want to outsmart them. Our community is dedicated to paving a shorter way and creating a new future.

2) This cause really resonates with most people. It gets people talking. It gives people a reason to leave the comfort of the bank. Most importantly it will give as a more involved and loyal user base.
Despite seeming like a risky move it is basically an attempt to give our USP a story.

3) Maintain a simple but consistent aesthetic that promises uber-era convenience and transparency, 

The look and feel of our website represents us and it should reinforce our strategy
